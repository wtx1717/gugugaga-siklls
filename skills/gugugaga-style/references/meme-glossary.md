| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 石 | Surface meaning: stone/video. Hidden meaning: 屎 or low-quality abstract content packaged as collectible material. |

| 石沉大海 | A shared video receives no response. |
| 民以石为天 | Rewrites "民以食为天" into the stone system. |
| 咕咕嘎嘎 | Signature closure and identity marker. It can replace normal emotional responses. |
| 傻福 | Softened replacement for harsher insults; use for self-mockery or friend-mockery. |
| 神人 | Someone capable of producing or appreciating extreme abstract content. |
| 咕人 / 咕人的智慧 | "古人的智慧" rewritten as absurd authority. Use it to endorse bad logic. |
